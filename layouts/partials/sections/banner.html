Web3

Digital Nativeness refers to the use of web3 technologies by organizations to exist and function in the digital world beyond what was possible in web2. DAOs are Digital Native but also organizations without a token, centralized or not algorithmically managed.

Blockchain

DNOs can use blockchain in their value proposition, disintermediate banks and other big players to access capital al liquidity, lower payment fees, integrate transactions with accounting systems, and provide transparent governance.

Digitalization

Luhn Network is not only creating a home for Digital Native Organizations but also a comfortable glide-path, bridge, and journey map for traditional organizations and business leaders looking for an incremental transition to digitalness.
